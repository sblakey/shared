## Ingredients

- 3 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 lb fresh Mexican chorizo
- 3 cups sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2 inch cubes
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt, divided
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
- 1 cup red onion, diced
- 1/4 cup water
- 1 cup sweet red bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 5 or 6 large eggs
- Garnish: clantro leaves, crushed red pepper, sliced jalepeno
- Toppings: queso fresco, salsa, etc.
- Tortillas

## Equipment

- Large 12" oven-safe skillet

## Prep
* Preheat oven to 350f
* Bowl A:
	* Peel and cut sweet potatoes to 1/2 inch cubes
	* 1/4 tsp kosher salt, cumin, and black pepper
* Bowl B:
	* Dice the red onion
* Bowl C:
	* Slice the bell pepper

## Pickup *(34-41 minutes)*
1. Heat the olive oil over **medium heat**. Crumble chorizo and cook until it begins to render fat, *5-7 minutes*.
2. Push the chorizo to the edges, create a well in the center. Add **Bowl A**. Cook *5-7 minutes*, unti lsweet potato is tender, gradually incorporating chorizo from edges
3. Add onion, **Bowl B**, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/4 cup water. **Deglaze**. Cook, stirring often, until onion is slightly softened, *3 minutes*.
4. Stir in red bell pepper **Bowl C**, cook *2 minutes*. Remove from heat.
5. Make wells, crack in eggs, season w/ salt to taste.
6. Bake to desired doneness, *12-15 minutes.*
7. Rest *5 minutes*, garnish.
