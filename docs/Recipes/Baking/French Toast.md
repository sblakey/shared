## Ingredients

*Custard, per 2-3 slices frozen bread*

- 1 egg
- 1/3 cup milk
- 1/2t vanilla
- 1/2t cinnamon
- pinch salt

*To grease pan*: 1T butter

## Process

1. Preheat pan to medium heat (6)
2. Whisk together
3. Soak in shallow pan or stasher bag, ~4 minutes
4. Melt buttern in pan
5. Fry ~2 minutes per side
