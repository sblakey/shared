# "Aunt Edith's Recipe"

*Was this Sara's Aunt Edith or Islah's?*

* 2 eggs
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 3T ice-cold water
* 1t vanilla
* 1/2t ground cardamom
* 1 stick butter, melted
* 1c flour

## Half-recipe

* 1 eggs
* 6T = 1/4 cup + 2T sugar
* 1 1/2T = 1T + 1.5t ice-cold water
* 1/2t vanilla
* 1/4t ground cardamom
* 1/2 stick butter, melted
* 1/2c flour
