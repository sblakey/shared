# Apple Butter

* 5 1/2 lb apples, cored (approx, fill pot)
* 2/3c water or apple juice
* 1c brown sugar
* 1 stick cinnamon
* 1/2t ground ginger
* 1/2 nutmeg

1. 15m in instant pot
2. Natural release
3. Blend
