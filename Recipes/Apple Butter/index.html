    Apple Butter¶

    • 5 1/2 lb apples, cored (approx, fill pot)
    • 2/3c water or apple juice
    • 1c brown sugar
    • 1 stick cinnamon
    • 1/2t ground ginger

    • 1/2 nutmeg

    • 15m in instant pot

    • Natural release
    • Blend
